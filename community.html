Community

More than just a community, a global network.

TechUp is a unique network that brings together passionate students and talented entrepreneurs united by one thing: technology, in a unique environment, events. We're a vibrant community, where curious and innovative minds come together to shape the future.

Within our community, we organize events that push the boundaries of imagination and technology. Our conferences, hackathons and trade shows attract the brightest minds in the industry, providing opportunities for networking, knowledge sharing and collaboration. We build a stimulating environment where ideas come to life, projects become reality and relationships are forged by drawing on a wide range of skills and perspectives.

TechUp is a world where the impossible becomes possible, where dreams come true, where passions are transformed into careers, and where the magic of the event creates the bond of success.
