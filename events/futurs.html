An innovative and total immersion

Let’s forget the preconceived ideas and assumptions that we associate with the future. On October 2nd at Station F, we invite you to participate in a concrete event, with real futuristic technologies that you can test yourself.

A personalized itinerary

More than 20 inventions await you at FUTURS. You can choose your path among the themes you like the most: conquest of space, health, culture, robotics, food, environment, and many more.

An exclusive experience

A large number of opportunities await you at Futurs: take part in a unique French event, meet a multitude of entrepreneurs present on site, and contribute to building the future.

An immersive event.

Our exhibitors

#3DFOOD

La Pâtisserie Numérique

La Pâtisserie Numérique is shaking up the world of pastry by allowing you to use 3D printing in sweet or savory creations. Come prepare and taste your own 3D printed recipes.

#HOLOGRAMS

Holusion

A specialist in digital signage, Holusion offers a wide range of holographic terminals and innovative displays offering holograms up to 2 meters.

#ROBOTICS

Eyepick

Eyepick is a hand gesture automation system that includes intelligent robotics and motion integration solutions. The robot arm can therefore observe you, copy you, and perform your own movements.

#CULTURE

Whatsit Consulting

Whatsit Consulting offers a new vision of the arts and live performance. Integrating digitalization and new technological tools, this start-up offers a new vision of the approach to Culture in all kinds.

#TRANSPORT

C.A.P.S.

C.A.P.S. presents the means of transport of the future: the aerial capsule. Designed by a trio of graduates of the École Normale Supérieure, this single-passenger and fully autonomous aircraft will be able to take you across Paris in less than 15 minutes. Want to come and discover a CAPS at Futurs?

#SPACETECH

ArianeWorks

A player in the acceleration of space innovations in Europe, ArianeWorks will be presenting its ideas and projects at the Futurs event to boost the next generations of Ariane rockets.

#COWORKING

La Vitre

For the first time, La Vitre lets you teleport to your meetings. An innovative device, this system allows you to collaborate endlessly: instant translation in 60 different languages, instant sharing of all types of content… La Vitre imposes no limits on you.

#MIXEDREALITY

Microsoft

HoloLens is the most comfortable and immersive mixed reality headset available. You will be able to discover how its use will transform the jobs of tomorrow. Who would have thought that we would push the limits of the real world with a headset?

#ART

Obvious

They are the French pioneers of crypto-art. After being the first French people to create an NFT painting, they will show you what this augmented creativity is all about. Want to see an Artificial Intelligence create a work of art? You already know where it's happening!

#FOOD

Les Nouveaux Fermiers

Les Nouveaux Fermiers are the first in France to reinvent meat using 100% plant-based ingredients. They offer a new, delicious diet that is healthy and better for the planet. Get ready for the tasting!

#AR

SMARTBOTTLE

Come and discover the Augmented Reality application for wine and spirits professionals. Without having to modify the label, SMARTBOTTLE transforms each bottle into a real communication medium for direct and unprecedented interaction.

#ESPORT

Mentalista Foot

Far from the frenzy of football stadiums, a mentalista football match takes place in a completely different atmosphere. 2 participants compete to move the ball with their imagination and concentration to make it roll and score in the opponent's goal.
