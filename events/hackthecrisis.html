Q&A

What is a hackathon?

A Hackathon is the gathering of motivated people, sharing the desire to solve important problems by pooling their various skills. They move quickly and come up with concrete ideas and concepts that can be implemented.

What is HackTheCrisis?

HackTheCrisis is a global movement born in Estonia, now present in 40+ countries around the world, referring to the organization of hackathons responding to concrete issues related to Covid-19. The mission: to have talents from all backgrounds & with various skills collaborate virtually in order to move from the idea to the useful prototype in 48 hours.

Who can participate?

Everyone can participate in Hack The Crisis 🇫🇷
Whether you want to embark on a new adventure, learn more or test your skills, we need you. 🤝💜

You can register with a team, or you can join a team.

I have an idea, what are the next steps?

Awesome ! Here are the next steps:
- Gather a team (if desired)
- Register (individually) & join the chat on Discord

What are conferences for?

To offer you an accelerated entrepreneurial experience, we have brought together all the ingredients necessary for your success at the event. The conferences aim to inspire you or develop new skills during the event.

What about intellectual property?

We do not claim any rights to your intellectual property, whatever it may be.
Everything you create belongs to you.

I don't know how to code, I must necessarily find a developer in my team?

No, you can very well have a team without a developer and use nocode tools to develop your prototype / MVP. In addition, plenty of mentors specialized in no-code will be there to coach you.

I have seen the mention 'Etudiants de la Tech' or ‘LET' several times in the replays, what is it?

‘Etudiants de la Tech’ (or LET) is the former name of TechUp non-profit organization. During this event, TechUp was still called ‘LET’ but on July 2023, ‘Etudiants de la Tech' has joined the TechUp constellation to merge under the name ‘TechUp Network’.
