Q&A

What is a hackathon?

A Hackathon is the gathering of motivated people, sharing the desire to solve important problems by pooling their various skills. They move quickly and come up with concrete ideas and concepts that can be implemented.

What is HackTheCrisis?

HackTheCrisis is a global movement born in Estonia, now present in 40+ countries around the world, referring to the organization of hackathons responding to concrete issues related to Covid-19. The mission: to have talents from all backgrounds & with various skills collaborate virtually in order to move from the idea to the useful prototype in 48 hours.
