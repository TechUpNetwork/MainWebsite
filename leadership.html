Paviel Schertzer

Chief Executive Officer
Paviel Schertzer is the Chief Executive Officer of TechUp. Before being named CEO in 2022, Paviel was the Chief Technical Officer (CTO) and Head of creative contents (photo/video) since 2018. He has worked on several events, including the HackTheCrisis France hackathon during the Covid pandemic, and several editions of Viva Technology and France Digitale events.

Paviel holds a Master's degree in Engineering from the School for Computing and Advanced Techniques (EPITA). He works full-time as a Project manager and Data engineer at IJFR, a research institute.

In his free time, he's a First Aid Manager at Protection Civile France, and a Partner at Upscale Organization. Previously, he was the General Secretary and Member of the Board of non-profit organization EpiMac.

Tom-Eliott Herfray

Executive Vice President
Tom-Eliott Herfray is the Executive Vice President of TechUp. Before being named VP in 2022, Tom-Eliott was the Chief Information Officer (CIO) and Tech Advisor, in charge of the organization's IT infrastructures since 2021.

Tom-Eliott holds a Master's degree in Engineering from the School for Computing and Advanced Techniques (EPITA). He works full-time as a Project Manager and Software Engineer at C2S, Bouygues Telecom Entreprises and Bouygues group’ digital services firm.

In his free time, he is a Partner at Upscale Organization, and previously was President and Chairman of the Board of the non-profit organization EpiMac.

Natacha Padova

Deputy Secretary
Natacha Padova is the Deputy Secretary of TechUp Network. Natacha joined the organization in 2022 after participating in the 2021 edition of Viva Technology.

Natacha holds a Master's degree in Engineering from the School for Computing and Advanced Techniques (EPITA). She works full-time as a Cloud & Engineering Analyst at Deloitte in Luxembourg.

Soren Szabo

Chief Financial Officer
Soren Szabo is the Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer of TechUp Network. Soren joined the organization in 2022 after having participated in many events as a photographer and video/photo editor for the communication team.

Soren holds a Master's degree in Game Design & Programming from ISART DIGITAL Paris. He works full-time as an Animation Programmer at Virtuos, and previously as a Gameplay programmer at Microids.

In his free time, he is a freelance photographer and video editor.

Vincent Attali

Chief Operations Officer
Vincent Attali is the Chief Operations Officer, Secretary and Data Privacy Officer of TechUp. Before being named Secretary in 2023, Vincent was TechUp's Community department manager. He was in charge of all the organization of events related to the community, and in particular the VivaTeam program launched in 2017 by TechUp and Viva Technology.

Vincent holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from the European Institute of Technology (Epitech). He works full-time as a Full-Stack Developer at Modjo.

Ieva Gaigala

Head of Board
